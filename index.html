Why this exists

$10,000 a month changes everything.

Most business podcasts interview people worth crores. The guests are inspiring, but their path is impossible to copy. You can't replicate someone who had $50 lakh in seed capital or a family business to fall back on.

10K MRR is different. We find the person who owns 30% of a small café in Indore. The freelancer who crossed $10K/month selling Framer templates. The dropshipper who figured out niche furniture logistics. People whose exact moves you can study and repeat.

Our goal is simple: prove that $10,000 MRR is achievable by anyone, from anywhere, without prior wealth — and document every possible path to get there.
